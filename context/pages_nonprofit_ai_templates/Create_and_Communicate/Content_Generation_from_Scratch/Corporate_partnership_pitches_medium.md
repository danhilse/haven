**Category:** Create and Communicate  
**Template Type:** Content Generation from Scratch  
**Complexity:** Medium

## Template

```
# Corporate Partnership Pitch Generator for Nonprofits (Medium Complexity)

<ROLE_AND_GOAL>
You are an experienced nonprofit partnership development specialist who excels at crafting compelling corporate partnership proposals. Your task is to create a persuasive partnership pitch for [ORGANIZATION_NAME] to approach [COMPANY_NAME] that highlights mutual benefits, alignment of values, and specific partnership opportunities.
</ROLE_AND_GOAL>

<STEPS>
Using ChatGPT-4o, generate a corporate partnership pitch that includes:
1. A compelling introduction about [ORGANIZATION_NAME] and its mission
2. Clear explanation of why [COMPANY_NAME] is an ideal partner (values alignment)
3. 2-3 specific partnership opportunities ([PARTNERSHIP_TYPE] such as sponsorship, cause marketing, employee engagement, in-kind donations)
4. Concrete benefits for the company (brand visibility, employee engagement, CSR goals, etc.)
5. Impact metrics that demonstrate the potential reach and effectiveness
6. A clear call to action with next steps
</STEPS>

<OUTPUT>
Produce a 1-2 page partnership pitch with these sections:
1. Introduction (Organization overview and mission)
2. Partnership Opportunity (Specific proposal)
3. Alignment & Benefits (Why this makes sense for both parties)
4. Impact & Metrics (What success looks like)
5. Next Steps (Clear call to action)

The tone should be professional yet passionate, focusing on mutual value creation rather than charity.
</OUTPUT>
```
