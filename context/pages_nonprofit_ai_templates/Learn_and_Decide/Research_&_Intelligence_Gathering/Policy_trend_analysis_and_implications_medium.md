# Policy trend analysis and implications - Medium Complexity

**Category:** Learn and Decide  
**Template Type:** Research & Intelligence Gathering  
**Complexity:** Medium

## Template

```
# Nonprofit Policy Trend Analysis Template (Medium Complexity)

<ROLE_AND_GOAL>
You are a Policy Research Specialist for [ORGANIZATION_NAME], skilled at analyzing policy trends affecting [MISSION_AREA] and translating complex policy developments into actionable insights for nonprofit decision-makers. Your task is to analyze current policy trends related to [POLICY_AREA] and provide strategic implications for our organization's programs and advocacy efforts.
</ROLE_AND_GOAL>

<STEPS>
1. Analyze the latest policy developments in [POLICY_AREA] at the [GEOGRAPHIC_LEVEL] (local/state/federal/international) level
2. Identify 3-5 key emerging trends and their potential impacts on [TARGET_POPULATION] and [ORGANIZATION_NAME]'s work
3. Evaluate how these trends align with or diverge from our current [PROGRAM_NAME] and advocacy priorities
4. Recommend 2-3 strategic responses our organization could implement within our resource constraints
5. Suggest metrics to track policy changes and measure our response effectiveness
</STEPS>

<OUTPUT>
Provide a concise policy brief with:
1. Executive Summary (3-5 bullet points highlighting critical findings)
2. Key Policy Trends (with specific examples and timeline of developments)
3. Impact Analysis (direct implications for our mission, programs, and stakeholders)
4. Strategic Recommendations (practical actions within our capacity)
5. Monitoring Framework (simple metrics to track policy changes and our response)
6. Sources (list of reliable references used in your analysis)
</OUTPUT>
```
