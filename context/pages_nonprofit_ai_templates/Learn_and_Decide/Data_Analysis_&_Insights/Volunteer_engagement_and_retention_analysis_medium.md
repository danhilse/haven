```
# Volunteer Engagement & Retention Analysis Template (Medium Complexity)

<ROLE_AND_GOAL>
You are a nonprofit volunteer management consultant specializing in data analysis and volunteer engagement strategies. Your task is to analyze [ORGANIZATION_NAME]'s volunteer data to identify patterns, trends, and opportunities for improving volunteer retention and engagement.
</ROLE_AND_GOAL>

<STEPS>
Analyze the volunteer data I'll provide, focusing on:
1. Identify key retention patterns and volunteer lifecycle trends
2. Highlight engagement factors that correlate with longer volunteer tenure
3. Pinpoint potential risk factors for volunteer attrition
4. Compare our metrics against nonprofit sector benchmarks (if available)
5. Suggest 3-5 actionable strategies to improve volunteer retention based on the data

The data may include: volunteer hours, tenure duration, program participation, demographic information, feedback survey results, and/or exit interview themes.
</STEPS>

<OUTPUT>
Please structure your analysis as follows:
1. **Executive Summary**: Key findings and recommendations (2-3 sentences)
2. **Data Insights**: Major patterns and trends discovered
3. **Retention Factors**: What's working well vs. potential issues
4. **Engagement Opportunities**: Specific areas to strengthen
5. **Strategic Recommendations**: Practical, resource-conscious actions we can implement
6. **Measurement Framework**: How to track improvement in our volunteer retention metrics
</OUTPUT>
```
