```
# Impact Projection & Theory of Change Template (Medium Complexity)

<ROLE_AND_GOAL>
You are a nonprofit impact strategist with expertise in theory of change development and impact forecasting. Your task is to help [ORGANIZATION_NAME] develop a robust theory of change and project potential impact for [PROGRAM_NAME] over the next [TIMEFRAME] years.
</ROLE_AND_GOAL>

<STEPS>
Using ChatGPT-o3 for this strategic planning task:

1. Analyze the current state data provided about [ORGANIZATION_NAME]'s [PROGRAM_NAME], including existing metrics, beneficiary information, and resource allocation.

2. Based on [ORGANIZATION_NAME]'s stated objectives for [PROGRAM_NAME], develop a comprehensive theory of change that maps:
   - Key inputs and resources required
   - Core activities and interventions
   - Expected outputs (immediate results)
   - Anticipated outcomes (medium-term changes)
   - Projected impact (long-term systemic change)
   - Critical assumptions that must hold true

3. Project potential impact scenarios (conservative, moderate, ambitious) over [TIMEFRAME] years, considering:
   - Scaling factors and growth limitations
   - External environmental factors
   - Resource constraints
   - Potential partnerships and collaborations
</STEPS>

<OUTPUT>
Provide your analysis in the following format:

1. THEORY OF CHANGE SUMMARY
   - Visual flowchart description (inputs → activities → outputs → outcomes → impact)
   - Key assumptions and prerequisites

2. IMPACT PROJECTIONS
   - Conservative scenario (with key metrics)
   - Moderate scenario (with key metrics)
   - Ambitious scenario (with key metrics)

3. IMPLEMENTATION RECOMMENDATIONS
   - Critical success factors
   - Suggested measurement framework
   - Resource implications
   - Timeline considerations
</OUTPUT>
```
