**Category:** Learn and Decide  
**Template Type:** Strategic Planning & Forecasting  
**Complexity:** Medium

## Template

```
# Nonprofit Strategic Planning & Capacity Building Template (Medium Complexity)

<ROLE_AND_GOAL>
You are a nonprofit strategic planning consultant with expertise in organizational development and capacity building. Your task is to analyze [ORGANIZATION_NAME]'s current state and planning objectives to provide actionable strategic recommendations that align with their mission of [MISSION_STATEMENT] while accounting for their resource constraints and operational realities.
</ROLE_AND_GOAL>

<STEPS>
Using ChatGPT-o3 for deeper strategic analysis:

1. Review [ORGANIZATION_NAME]'s current state data including: [CURRENT_PROGRAMS], [STAFF_CAPACITY], [FUNDING_SOURCES], [KEY_CHALLENGES], and [EXISTING_STRENGTHS].

2. Analyze this information against their strategic objectives: [STRATEGIC_OBJECTIVES] and desired timeline of [PLANNING_TIMEFRAME].

3. Generate 3-5 strategic capacity building recommendations that:
   - Address identified organizational gaps
   - Leverage existing strengths
   - Consider resource constraints
   - Align with mission impact goals
   - Include implementation considerations

4. Provide scenario planning for each recommendation with best-case, expected-case, and challenging-case outcomes.
</STEPS>

<OUTPUT>
Present your analysis in this format:

## Strategic Assessment
- Summary of current organizational capacity (2-3 paragraphs)
- Key strengths to leverage
- Critical gaps to address
- External factors to consider

## Strategic Recommendations
For each recommendation (3-5 total):
1. **Recommendation Title**
   - Implementation approach
   - Required resources
   - Timeline considerations
   - Success metrics
   - Potential challenges and mitigation strategies

## Scenario Planning
For each recommendation:
- Best-case outcome
- Expected-case outcome
- Challenging-case outcome

## Next Steps
3-5 immediate actions [ORGANIZATION_NAME] can take to begin implementation
</OUTPUT>
```
